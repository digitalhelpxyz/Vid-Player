Privacy Policy

Vid Player Android Application

This Privacy Policy explains how Vid Player ("we", "our", or "us") handles user data and privacy when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Vid Player does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or precise location.

The app accesses media files stored on your device only to provide core functionality such as video and audio playback.

Permissions We Use

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO
    Used to access and play video and audio files stored on your device.
  • READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED
    Allows access to user-selected media files on Android 14+ devices.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Legacy)
    Used for backward compatibility on older Android versions.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS
    Used to show playback notifications (e.g., background audio controls).
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
    Required for controlling audio playback behavior.
  • INTERNET & NETWORK ACCESS
    Used for Chromecast casting and loading ads.
  • AD_ID (Advertising ID)
    Used by Google AdMob to provide relevant ads.

How We Use Your Data

All media files remain on your device. Vid Player does not upload, store, or share your personal files on any external server.

Advertising (AdMob)

Vid Player uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect limited data such as device identifiers (Advertising ID) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted services such as Google Play Services and Chromecast functionality. These services may collect data as per their own policies.

Data Security

Your media files stay on your device. We do not store or transmit personal data externally.

Children’s Privacy

Vid Player is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
