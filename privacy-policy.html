Privacy Policy

Vid Player – Android Application

This Privacy Policy describes how Vid Player ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

Vid Player does not collect personal information such as name, phone number, email address, or location data from users.

The application may access media files stored on your device in order to display and play videos. This access is required for the core functionality of the video player.

Permissions Used

Vid Player may request certain permissions to provide its features:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / Storage Permission
    Used to scan and display video files stored on your device so you can play them inside the app.

How We Use Information

Any accessed media files remain on your device. Vid Player does not upload, store, or share your personal media files on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Vid Player may use trusted third-party services such as Google Play services to improve app performance and reliability. These services may collect limited information as governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user privacy. Since the application primarily works offline and processes files locally on your device, your personal media files remain under your control.

Children’s Privacy

Vid Player is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
