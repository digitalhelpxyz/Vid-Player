About Vid Player

Vid Player is a powerful and lightweight Android video player designed to play all video formats smoothly. Built using the advanced LibVLC media engine, Vid Player delivers high-performance video playback including 4K, Full HD, and Ultra HD videos.

Key Features

All Video Formats

Supports MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WMV and many more formats.

4K / Ultra HD Playback

Watch high quality 4K and Full HD videos with smooth hardware acceleration.

LibVLC Engine

Advanced LibVLC playback engine ensures powerful media decoding.

Subtitle Support

Load and sync subtitles easily while watching movies.

Background Play

Continue audio playback while using other apps.

Gesture Controls

Control brightness, volume and seek easily using gestures.

Why Choose Vid Player?

Vid Player is designed for users who want a powerful, fast and reliable video player. With support for almost all media formats and optimized performance using LibVLC technology, Vid Player delivers the best video playback experience on Android phones and tablets.
